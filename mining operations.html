Causes:Mining Operations Mining is a process wherein
minerals below the earth are extracted
using large equipments.
During the process dust and chemicals
are released in the air causing
large amount of air pollution.
This is one of the reason that damaged
workers and nearby residents health. Mining provides iron and copper
for making aeroplanes, refrigerators.
Mines also supply salt for food, gold,
silver and diamonds
for jewellery;
and coal for fuel. We mine uranium for
nuclear energy, stone for buildings,
phosphate for fertilizers and gravel for
roads.
